Contact Us

Fluffytails.com is a searchable list of pets from thousands of shelters and foster groups. Begin your search using our Pet Search (at the top of this page). Since Petfinder is updated on a daily basis, we recommend you keep checking back or save your query so we’ll send you an automatic e-mail when new pets are added that match your search (after you search for a pet, click on “Save This Query” at the bottom of the page).

We have specially trained staff dedicated to answering questions from shelter workers and placement groups. If you are a SHELTER or PLACEMENT GROUP and you need to know how to use fluffytails.com or need technical assistance about your pet account,

For instance, what is the adoption fee for a pet? Can you adopt out of state? Please visit our adoption policies page for detailed information.

If you experience any issues entering your location, try using a different browser or clearing your browser's cache. For further assistance, contact support.

You can link to our site using our pre-designed banners and badges available on the Link to Us page.

Please contact the shelter or rescue organization listed with the pet for the most accurate and up-to-date information.

Ensure you check your spam/junk email folder. If you still don’t see a reply, try contacting the organization directly.

Please share your concerns with the appropriate authorities or reach out to us for guidance.

Still have a question? For all other inquires, send us an email.
